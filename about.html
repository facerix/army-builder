About Personnel

Personnel was built using standard web technologies by me, Rylee Corradini. It's a Progressive Web App, meaning you can install it directly to your tablet/phone screen/desktop like a regular app, and it will behave more or less the same as one, and save its data in your device storage - no login needed.

I built Personnel because I wanted an easy, free way to build and track my army lists for Warhammer 40k and Age of Sigmar. It's modelled (loosely) after the official Games Workshop apps, but with a focus on simplicity, ease of use, and cost. It's also a work in progress, so expect bugs and missing features.

Most art assets are from SVG Repo; placeholder animations were generated using SVGBackgrounds.com. Some AoS & 40k icons are from Warhammer40kGroup; the faction icons are from Certseeds. Additional 40k-flavor icons are from Locequen. Most were tweaked by me, because SVG is rad that way.
